About Nawaz Hotel

Our Story

Nawaz Hotel opened its doors in 2013 with a simple goal: give travellers a clean, comfortable, and affordable place to stay without cutting corners on service. What started as a small twelve-room property has grown into a full-service hotel with rooms for every kind of traveller, from solo business visitors to families on holiday.
